Since July 2022 retired from Eawag: Swiss Federal Institute of Aquatic Science and Technoloy but still contributing to research in selected projects

Former institute: Eawag: Swiss Federal Institute of Aquatic Science and Technoloy, http://www.eawag.ch/en
Former department: Systems Analysis, Integrated Assessment, and Modelling, http://www.eawag.ch/en/department/siam

Main research fields:

    focus on Bayesian inference

  • Hydrological, biogeochemical and ecological modelling:
